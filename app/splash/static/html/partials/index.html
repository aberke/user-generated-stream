About

tldr; Create mobile-friendly slideshow and polls, recieve embed code, embed widget on your page.

Slideshows swipe from one slide to the next in sequence, while a swipe on a poll is a tinderesque gesture that signifies either an upvote (swipe-left), or a downvote (swipe-right).

The editor can either pull slides for the slideshow/poll from a #campaign on twitter or instagram, or they can make custom slides.

What distinguishes this from the current slideshows is that there's the option for anyone to submit their own content via a post to twitter/instagram with your slideshow/poll's #title. Also, it performs nicely on mobile.

The sales team is currently using this product for selling sponsored content. For example, PetSmart is running a campaigin #inspiredbypets and wants a slideshow with the best submissions.

I'm seeking feedback for:

  • How this should share to twitter and facebook
  • What this widget should look like
  • What copy (text) would make the widget's function clearest to your readers
  • Any other insights!
